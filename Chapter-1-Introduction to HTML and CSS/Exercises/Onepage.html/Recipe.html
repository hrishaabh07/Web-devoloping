Cheesy Baked Pasta

Introduction

This Cheesy Baked Pasta is the ultimate comfort food. Packed with layers of rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and lots of melted cheese, it's perfect for cozy nights or weekend gatherings!

Ingredients

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups tomato pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil (optional)

Method

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add tomato sauce, chili flakes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Combine the cooked pasta with the sauce. Mix in half of the mozzarella and parmesan.
  5. Transfer to a baking dish. Top with remaining cheese.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  7. Garnish with basil, serve hot and cheesy!
